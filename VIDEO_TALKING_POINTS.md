# FINAL TIPS FOR WINNING

1. **Energy matters**: Judges watch many videos. Be enthusiastic but genuine.

2. **Show, don't tell**: When possible, let the UI speak. Don't over-explain.

3. **The debate is your star**: Spend extra time here - it's your unique innovation.

4. **SDG connection**: Judges are looking for sustainability impact. Emphasize the quantification.

5. **Technical confidence**: Mention "7 agents", "LLM-powered", "blockchain audit trail" - show you built something real.

6. **Finish strong**: The last 10 seconds are what judges remember. Nail the closing.
